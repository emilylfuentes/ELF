Who Is Em Fuentes?

Hello, I'm Emily!

I am currently a senior at The College of New Jersey studying Communications with a concentration in Radio/Television/Film and a minor in Graphic Design. I work closely with the Communications Department at The College of New Jersey with things such as the Student Advisory Board and being a student media technician.

My goal in life is to work in Post Production as a commerical/short form editor. I am currently interning in NYC at a Post Production company, Spot Welders Inc.

I enjoy spending time with my family and going on adventures with my friends. I dream of traveling the world with places like Ireland, Germany, Spain and Greece at the top of my list.
